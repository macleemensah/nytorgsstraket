# Nytorgsstråket Brand Guidelines

This document outlines the core brand guidelines for **Nytorgsstråket** based on the official brand book to ensure visual consistency across the website and any digital properties.

## 1. Logo Usage
The Nytorgsstråket logo is the primary visual identifier.
- **Available Variants:** Red Logo and Black Logo.
- Always maintain clear space around the logo to ensure visibility, elegance, and impact.

## 2. Color Palette
The color palette balances classic elegance with striking highlights.
| Color | Usage |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Brand Red** | Used for the primary logo variant, prominent accents, and key interactive elements. |
| **Brand Black** | Used for high-contrast typography, the black logo variant, and stark UI details. |
| **White/Off-White** | Used as the primary canvas to make the typography and red accents stand out. |

## 3. Typography
The typography system relies on a mix of elegant serifs, modern condensed sans-serifs, and expressive script accents.

**1. Primary Typeface: Orpheus Pro**
- **Usage:** Main headings (`h1`, `h2`), large expressive text, and elegant editorial paragraphs.
- **Style Note:** Imparts a Nordic theatre of sartorial wit and urban poise.

**2. Secondary Typeface: DIN Condensed**
- **Usage:** Utility text, addresses (e.g., *NYTORGSGATAN # 36-38 STOCKHOLM SWEDEN*), subheaders, and structural layout tags.
- **Style Note:** Clean, modern, and provides a structural contrast to the serif.

**3. Accent / Exclamation Typeface: Lindstaye**
- **Usage:** Special callouts, playful slogans, and expressive short texts.
- **Examples:** *"Trevligt att stråkas!", "New kid in the hood!", "SoFo-Nights!"*

## 4. Brand Voice & Messaging
Incorporate these core concepts, slogans, and structural ideas where appropriate throughout the design:

**Key Slogans:**
- *"Något att älska"* (Something to love)
- *"Något att prata om"* (Something to talk about)
- *"Något att komma ihåg"* (Something to remember)
- *"Något att dela med sig om"* (Something to share)
- *"Compact Shopping"*
- *"Shop 'n Stroll"*
- *"Boutique"*

**Associated Promenade Brands:**
Acne Studios, Aesop, A.P.C., Pärlans, House of Tell, Lysine Bleu, Lykke.
